About

I am a freelance developer based in Kyoto, Japan. I've been living here for 3 years working part-time. Staying in Japan has greatly influenced my work-ethic and determination. Finishing projects on due time, delivering great-quality and effective results are some of the traits that I pursue to possess and promise to deliver.

I'm addicted to learning new frameworks, programming languages and a whole bunch of other things, which makes me updated to all the new things in the tech industry, giving me the advantage to innovate, do something "cool" or unusual, but effective solutions for my client's projects and requests.
My main goal is to help my clients improve and sky-rocket their businesses through my effective strategy, planning and goal setting mindset.
